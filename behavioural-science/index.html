Behavioural science

Behavioural Science (BehSci) is a multidisciplinary approach to the study of human behaviour and decision-making, combining the findings and methods from psychology, neuroscience, and other social sciences.

Learn more

What choices are people making? How are they behaving? Why?

Behavioural scientists ask these questions and design and test solutions based on that understanding.

The Impact and Innovation Unit (IIU) applies insights from BehSci to inform the design and implementation of priority programs, services, and initiatives across government. From improving Canadians’ financial health, to improving environmental outcomes, to slowing the spread of COVID-19, we are leveraging our expertise to drive better outcomes for all Canadians.
